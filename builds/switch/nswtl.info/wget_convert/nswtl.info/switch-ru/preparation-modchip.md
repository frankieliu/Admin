This guide prepares you for modchip installation. Modchipping is required for all patched consoles and provides permanent hacking capability.

## What is a Modchip?

A modchip is a hardware device installed on your Switch's motherboard that enables hacking on previously un-hackable consoles.

### Types of Modchips

1. **Glitch Chips** (for all consoles):
   - HWFly / PicoFly
   - Picofly / RP2040
   - SX Core / SX Lite

2. **RCM Chips** (for unpatched consoles):
   - Convenience only
   - Replaces jig + injector
   - Not required for hacking

## Before You Begin

### ⚠️ Important Warnings

- **Warranty Void**: Modchipping voids your warranty
- **Risk of Damage**: Installation requires soldering
- **Professional Help**: Recommended unless experienced
- **Backup First**: Always backup saves before sending
