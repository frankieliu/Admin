Backing up and restoring saves
You can find most of the answers to typical questions in the FAQ section.
If something goes wrong, don't panic! First, read the Problems and Solutions section.
If you are a MacOS user, use these tips to avoid memory card problems.
Make backups of your saves as often as possible!
If the console freezes or shows no signs of life, turn it off by holding the (POWER) button for 30 seconds, then turn it on and forward the payload!
Creating and restoring backups via MTP
Connecting the console to the PC
Connect the console to the PC using a USB cable (Type-C)
On your console, launch DBI using the Homebrew Launcher.
Click “ Run MTP Responder ”
A new MTP device will be mounted to your PC.
Open the “ Switch ” device that appears on your computer (in “This PC” on Windows)
Creating backup copies of saves
Go to the “ Saves ” drive and copy the folders  Installed games  to  Uninstalled games  your PC .
To back up a specific installed game , go to the folder  Installed games  and copy the game's folder to your PC. Look for the save file for an uninstalled game in the folder.  Uninstalled games 
If you're looking for a backup for a specific user, go to the folder with the game's name and you'll see backups for all users on the console.
Restoring backup saves
To restore a save using this method, the game must have been launched at least once on your console.

Go to the “ Saves ” ->  Installed games  ->  %название игры%  -> drive  %имя_пользователя%  and copy the data that is in the corresponding folder of your backup to this folder.
This way you can copy backup copies of saves created by other programs, or restore other people's saves.
