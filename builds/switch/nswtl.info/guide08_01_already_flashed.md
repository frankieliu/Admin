Part II - Running Hekate with Caffeine
On the PegaScape page, select the Caffeine icon
The spinner on the screen will freeze, but the one in the lower left corner will spin. Be patient! On average, it takes about 45 seconds from the moment you click the icon until the exploit is triggered.
If the spinner freezes for more than a minute, reboot the device by holding the power button for 30 seconds and try again. You should also do the same if the screen suddenly turns white.
Wait for the text field to appear
Click on the text field and count to five
Press the power button
If everything is done correctly, the set-top box will reboot into the custom firmware.

Possible problems and their solutions

Launch of HBL
HBL is a program for launching custom applications. Typically, these are system utilities and emulators, and less commonly, games. These applications are distributed in the .exe format  .nro  . To install, place  .nro  the application in the .exe folder  switch  . In this guide, all applications discussed are already included in the kefir package.

For Atmosphere users

Launch any game and hold down the (R) key to enter HBL
Hold until HBL appears (yes, even in the profile selection menu)
If there are no games, launch the album by holding (R). However, it is still recommended to launch it through a game when one appears.
You can still use albums, but it's recommended to launch apps through the game. This way, apps will have access to the console's full memory.
