General information
Currently, chipping is the only way to flash a Mariko- based Switch.▼ or patched Erista, that is, all consoles that are not susceptible to hacking via Fusée Gelée, as well as Switch Lite.

Chipping is a hardware hack. This means installing a chip by soldering it to the console's motherboard. Therefore, if your console still has an official warranty, installing a chip will void it.

The system software version of your set-top box does not matter.

Will it work online?
A flashed console can run either custom or official firmware. With custom firmware, you only have access to local online play between two consoles, or pirated online play. However, by switching to official firmware, you can also use regular online play. Keep in mind that online play will only work with officially downloaded or purchased games.

Which memory card should I choose for a flashed console?
A card from a reputable brand, purchased from a reputable store rather than a cheap one, and of a class no lower than U1. After purchasing, be sure to check the card for authenticity.
